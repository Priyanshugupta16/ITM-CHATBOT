Quick Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the college office hours? ▼
The college office is open Monday to Saturday, 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM. The campus remains accessible to residential students 24/7.
How long does it take to get an admission response? ▼
After submitting your application online, the admissions team typically responds within 2–3 working days. You can also call 0751-2496701 for faster assistance.
Can I visit the campus before applying? ▼
Yes! Campus visits are welcome. Please call the admissions office at least one day in advance to schedule a guided tour. Walk-ins are also accepted during office hours.
Is there an option to chat with a counsellor? ▼
Yes. You can use our AI chatbot for instant answers, or if you need personal counselling, contact the admissions office via phone or email and a counsellor will connect with you.
How can I get a college brochure? ▼
The college brochure is available for download at itmuniversity.ac.in. You can also request a printed copy by emailing info@itmuniversity.ac.in or visiting the college office.
