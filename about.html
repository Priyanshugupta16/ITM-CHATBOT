Who we are

Excellence in
Technical Education

ITM University, Gwalior is one of Madhya Pradesh's leading private universities, offering world-class education in Engineering, Management, Computer Science, and more. With a sprawling campus and modern infrastructure, ITM has been grooming professionals for over 25 years.

Affiliated with RGPV and approved by AICTE, ITM maintains high academic standards with experienced faculty, research-focused labs, and strong industry connections for placements.

College History

25+ years of building careers and changing lives

1997
Established ITM College
Founded with BTech programs in Engineering under RGPV affiliation in Gwalior, MP.
2005
Management School Launched
MBA program introduced with specializations in Marketing, Finance, and HR.
2012
University Status Granted
Elevated to ITM University by Government of Madhya Pradesh. New campus expanded.
2018
Research & Innovation Centre
Launched R&D labs for AI, IoT, and Robotics. Industry tie-ups with TCS, Infosys, and more.
